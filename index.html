Biography

Zhang San is an Associate Professor in the Department of Electronic Engineering at XXX University. His research centers on energy-efficient computing hardware beyond the Moore era, including in-memory/near-memory computing accelerators, neuromorphic chips, and photonic-electronic integrated systems. He has published over 80 papers in top-tier venues (ISSCC, JSSC, Nature Electronics, etc.), with more than 5,000 citations (Google Scholar) and an h-index of 35.
